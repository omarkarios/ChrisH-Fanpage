Recent roles

Chris Hemsworth's most famous role is as the god Thor in the Marvel "Avengers" film series. He has starred in three individual Thor films and co-starred alongside Robert Downey Jr., Chris Evans and Scarlett Johansson in ensemble multi-hero Avengers films.




Upcoming Projects

Chris Hemsworth is set to feature in Furiosa, Transformer's One, Extraction 3 and the Hulk Hogan Biopic.
